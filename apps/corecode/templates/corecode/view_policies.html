Introduction

Purpose: The purpose of this policy manual is to outline the standards and guidelines that govern the academic, administrative, clinical, and research activities at [Medical School Name]. These policies are designed to ensure the highest quality of education, promote a safe and respectful environment, and uphold the ethical standards of the medical profession.

Scope: This manual applies to all students, faculty, staff, and affiliated personnel at [Medical School Name].
