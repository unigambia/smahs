Academic Advising

Overview

Purpose: Academic advising helps students to navigate their educational pathways, make informed decisions about their courses, and achieve their academic goals.

Services Provided: Course selection guidance, academic performance review, degree requirements clarification, and academic planning.

How to Access: Schedule an appointment with an academic advisor through the student portal or visit the advising office during drop-in hours.

Counseling Services

Mental Health and Wellness

Purpose: Counseling services support the mental health and emotional well-being of students. Services include individual counseling, group therapy, and workshops on stress management, anxiety, and other topics.

Confidentiality: All counseling sessions are confidential, and information is not shared without the student's consent.

How to Access: Contact the counseling center to schedule an appointment or attend walk-in hours for immediate support.

Career Services

Career Development and Job Placement

Purpose: Career services assist students in exploring career options, preparing for the job market, and securing employment. Services include resume reviews, mock interviews, job search strategies, and networking opportunities.

Workshops and Events: Attend workshops and career fairs to enhance your job search skills and connect with potential employers.

How to Access: Schedule an appointment with a career advisor through the career services portal or attend drop-in hours.

Financial Aid

Financial Assistance and Scholarships

Purpose: The financial aid office provides assistance to students in securing financial resources to support their education. Services include information on scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study programs.

Application Process: Submit the application form for student aid available at the university's financial aid office. Additionally, provide necessary documents such as proof of enrollment, academic transcripts, and any other required documentation to be considered for financial aid.

How to Access: Visit the financial aid office or schedule an appointment with a financial aid advisor for personalized assistance.

Health Services

Student Health and Wellness

Purpose: Health services provide comprehensive healthcare to students, including primary care, preventive care, and wellness programs.

Services Provided: Medical consultations, immunizations, lab tests, mental health services, and health education.

How to Access: Schedule an appointment online or visit the student health center during operating hours. Emergency services are available 24/7.
