Access to Facilities

General Access

Eligibility: All students, faculty, and staff are eligible for access to campus facilities during operating hours. Identification badges must be worn at all times.

Hours of Operation: The facilities are open from 6 AM to 10 PM on weekdays and from 8 AM to 8 PM on weekends.

Access Procedures: Swipe your ID card at the entrance to gain access. Report any issues with access to the campus security office.

Library Access

Library Services

Eligibility: All enrolled students, faculty, and staff have access to the library and its resources.

Operating Hours: The library is open from 8 AM to 11 PM on weekdays and from 9 AM to 9 PM on weekends.

Borrowing Materials: Present your ID card at the circulation desk to borrow books and other materials. Online resources can be accessed using your institutional login credentials.

IT Services

IT Support and Services

Eligibility: IT services are available to all students, faculty, and staff.

Help Desk: The IT help desk is available 24/7 for technical support. Contact them at (123) 456-7890 or it-support@medschool.edu.

Services Provided: IT services include email support, software installations, network access, and troubleshooting hardware issues.

Clinical Lab Access

Access to Clinical Laboratories

Eligibility: Access to clinical labs is restricted to students enrolled in relevant courses, faculty, and authorized staff.

Safety Protocols: All individuals must complete safety training before accessing the labs. Follow all posted safety procedures and wear appropriate protective gear.

Access Procedures: Schedule lab time through the lab coordinator. Swipe your ID card at the lab entrance to gain access.

Security and Privacy

Maintaining Security and Privacy

Data Security: All users must adhere to data security protocols to protect personal and institutional information. Report any security breaches immediately to the IT department.

Privacy Policies: Respect the privacy of fellow students, faculty, and staff. Personal information should not be shared without consent.

Secure Access: Use strong passwords for all institutional accounts and do not share your login credentials with others.
