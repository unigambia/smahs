Support Overview

About Our Support Services

The School of Medicine and Allied Health Sciences support team is dedicated to assisting students, faculty, and staff with any issues or questions they may have. Our goal is to provide prompt and effective support to ensure a smooth and efficient experience with our systems and services.

Contact Methods

How to Reach Us

Phone Support: Call us at (123) 456-7890 for immediate assistance. Our phone lines are open during support hours.

Email Support: Send an email to support@medschool.edu.gm Please include a detailed description of your issue and any relevant screenshots or attachments.

Live Chat: Use our live chat feature available on the support portal for real-time assistance.

In-Person Support: Visit our support office located in Building B, Room 203 during support hours for face-to-face assistance.

Support Hours

When We Are Available

Monday to Friday: 8 AM to 6 PM

Saturday: 9 AM to 3 PM

Sunday: Closed

For urgent issues outside of these hours, please use our online support portal to submit a ticket, and we will respond as soon as possible.

Common Issues

Frequently Addressed Problems

If you need to reset your password, visit our password reset portal and follow the instructions. For additional help, contact support by phone or email.
To connect to the campus Wi-Fi, select "MedSchoolWiFi" and log in using your institutional credentials. Detailed instructions can be found on our Wi-Fi setup page.
Required software for your courses can be downloaded from our software portal. Use your institutional credentials to log in and access the downloads.
