How to Schedule an Appointment

To schedule an appointment with an advisor, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the student portal.
  2. Navigate to the "Advising" section.
  3. Select your advisor and choose an available time slot.
  4. Confirm your appointment and check your email for a confirmation message.
Drop-In Hours

If you need immediate assistance, you can visit the advising office during drop-in hours. No appointment is necessary:

  • Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
  • Monday to Thursday: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
