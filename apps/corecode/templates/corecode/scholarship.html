Financial Aid and Scholarship Information
Learn about financial aid and scholarship opportunities available to you.

The financial aid office is here to help you secure the financial resources you need to support your education. We provide information on scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study programs.

Services Provided:

  • Information on available scholarships and grants.
  • Assistance with student loan applications.
  • Guidance on work-study opportunities.

How to Access:

  • Submit the application form for student aid available at the university's financial aid office.
  • Provide necessary documents such as proof of enrollment, academic transcripts, and any other required documentation.
  • Schedule an appointment with a financial aid advisor for personalized assistance.
  • Visit the Financial Aid page for more information.
