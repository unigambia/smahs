General Information

Overview

The IT Help Desk is dedicated to providing technical support and services to students, faculty, and staff at the School of Medicine and Allied Health Sciences. Our goal is to ensure that all users have access to the resources they need to effectively perform their academic and administrative tasks.

Services Provided

Technical Support

Software and Hardware Support: Assistance with installation, troubleshooting, and maintenance of software and hardware.

Network and Connectivity Issues: Support for Wi-Fi connectivity, VPN access, and network-related problems.

Account and Password Management: Help with account creation, password resets, and access issues.

IT Training and Workshops: Training sessions and workshops on various IT topics, including software usage and cybersecurity best practices.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

To reset your password, visit the password reset portal and follow the instructions. If you need further assistance, contact the IT Help Desk.
To connect to the campus Wi-Fi, select the network named "MedSchoolWiFi" and log in with your institutional credentials. For detailed instructions, visit our Wi-Fi setup page.
Required software for courses can be downloaded from the software portal. You will need to log in with your institutional credentials to access the downloads.
